# Purpose

This document defines the standard user journey across the entire NorthStar product.

Every module should follow this journey unless an approved exception exists.

NorthStar adapts to the user's problem.

The user should never have to understand the internal architecture of the product.

---

# Universal User Journey

## Step 1 — Start

The user enters NorthStar through a clear entry point.

Examples:

- Housing Loans
- Leasing
- Financial Terms
- Affordability
- Document Analysis

---

## Step 2 — Understand the Situation

NorthStar first understands the user's situation.

The product asks only the information needed.

Never overwhelm the user.

---

## Step 3 — Learn

Whenever possible,

NorthStar explains the topic before requesting decisions.

Learning is part of every journey.

---

## Step 4 — Explore

Users can:

- ask AI
- browse explanations
- use calculators
- upload documents

The journey adapts to the user's needs.

---

## Step 5 — Understand

NorthStar summarizes the key information.

Users should feel more confident than when they started.

---

## Step 6 — Continue

NorthStar suggests the next logical learning step.

Never force the next action.

Always leave the decision to the user.

---

# Journey Principles

Every journey should be:

- simple
- educational
- calm
- trustworthy
- flexible

---

# Success Criteria

The journey succeeds when users understand their financial situation better than before.
