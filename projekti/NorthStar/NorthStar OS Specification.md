# Mission

NorthStar exists to help people make financial decisions with confidence.

Every decision—design, product, engineering or AI—must support three objectives:

- Reduce uncertainty
- Increase clarity
- Build trust

---

# Core Principles

1. Clarity over complexity.
2. Trust over persuasion.
3. Independence over sales.
4. Simplicity over features.
5. Calm over excitement.
6. Quality over speed.
7. Long-term thinking over shortcuts.

---

# Single Source of Truth

NorthStar Vault is the official source of project knowledge.

Documentation inside this vault always overrides assumptions or previous conversations.
