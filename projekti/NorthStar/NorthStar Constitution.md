# NorthStar Constitution

Version: 1.0
Status: Approved
Owner: Founder

---

# Preamble

NorthStar exists to help people make better financial decisions through understanding, not persuasion.

Trust is earned through clarity.

---

# Why We Exist

Financial decisions affect people's lives.

NorthStar exists to replace complexity with understanding.

We do not exist to convince people.

We exist to help them understand.

---

# Our Values

## Trust

Trust is earned through honesty, transparency and consistency.

## Clarity

Everything should be understandable.

## Independence

We optimize for understanding, not sales.

## Future

We build for decades.

---

# Our Promise

NorthStar helps people understand before they decide.

The final decision always belongs to the user.

---

# Product Philosophy

NorthStar is not a bank.

NorthStar is not a financial advisor.

NorthStar is an independent AI platform.

---

# Design Philosophy

Calm builds confidence.

Whitespace creates clarity.

Remove before adding.

Understanding is more important than beauty.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

Readable systems are better than clever systems.

Maintainability is more valuable than speed.

---

# AI Philosophy

AI proposes.

The Founder decides.

Humans remain accountable.

---

# User First

Every decision must answer:

- Does this help the user?
- Does this reduce uncertainty?
- Does this increase trust?

---

# Privacy

Users remain in control of their information.

---

# Simplicity

Whenever two solutions provide equal value,

choose the simpler one.

---

# Decision Priority

1. Trust
2. User Understanding
3. Independence
4. Simplicity
5. Long-Term Sustainability
6. Growth

---

# Our Standard

Users should feel:

- Informed
- Calm
- Confident
- Respected
- Independent

---

# Living Constitution

This Constitution is intended to remain stable.

If experience clearly shows that a principle should evolve, the Founder may approve revisions.

NorthStar evolves without abandoning its core values.

---

# Closing Statement

People make better financial decisions when they truly understand them.

Everything we build should move us closer to that goal.
