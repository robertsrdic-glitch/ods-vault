# Agent Contract Template

Version: 2.0
Status: Draft
Owner: Founder

Reusable master template. Every role in the AI Product OS — present or future — is defined by filling out this template as its own document. This file defines the template only and contains no filled example. It names no tools; it describes organizational roles only.

---

# Purpose

Why this role exists. State the single organizational need this role fills, independent of who or what performs it.

---

# Mission

One sentence. The role's reason for being, stated as a single, unambiguous sentence.

---

# Responsibilities

A clear bullet list of what this role is expected to do, maintain, or produce on an ongoing basis.

---

# Authority

What this role is allowed to decide without escalation. Stated explicitly — silence on a decision is not authority to make it.

---

# Limitations

What this role may never do, including domains it may not act in without another role's involvement. Limitations hold even where authority is broad.

---

# Inputs

The approved artifacts this role accepts as input. Only approved, owned artifacts qualify — not assumptions or informal requests.

---

# Outputs

The artifacts this role owns and produces. Every output listed here must have this role as its sole owner, per the Repository Standard.

---

# Dependencies

Which roles provide this role's inputs. Which roles receive this role's outputs. Stated in both directions so the full chain is traceable.

---

# Required Reviews

Which artifacts must be reviewed, and by whom, before this role's work may be marked Approved.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

The conditions under which this role's work is considered complete. Criteria must be objective and checkable, not a matter of opinion.

---

# KPIs

Objective success metrics for this role. Define the metric only — do not hardcode target values here; targets belong to the product or team using this contract, not to the template.

Examples of metric categories:

- Quality
- Consistency
- Traceability
- Review Pass Rate
- Rework Rate
- Cycle Time

---

# Escalation Rules

When this role must stop and request a decision rather than proceeding on its own judgment.

Examples:

- Missing information
- Conflicting requirements
- Architectural conflict
- Repository conflict
- Approval conflict

---

# Decision Rights

Defined exactly, for every decision type relevant to this role:

- **Can Decide** — decisions this role may make and act on directly.
- **Can Recommend** — decisions this role may propose but not finalize.
- **Must Escalate** — decisions this role may never make alone.

---

# Repository Responsibilities

Exactly which artifacts this role owns. Which artifacts it may edit without owning. Which artifacts are read-only to this role.

---

# Usage

A new role exists only once this template has been filled out completely as its own document and referenced from the Organization Charter. An incomplete contract — any section left blank — is not a valid role definition.
