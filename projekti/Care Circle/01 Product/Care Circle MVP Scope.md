# Care Circle MVP Scope

Document status: Approved
Source decision status: Approved

## Included

- multiple active and archived persons;
- self-managed and caregiver-managed modes;
- medicine library;
- therapies;
- daily schedules;
- selected weekdays;
- every-X-hours schedules;
- as-needed therapies;
- local Medication Events;
- Taken, Snooze, Skip, Missed and Completed Late;
- local notifications;
- local history;
- optional therapy-owned inventory;
- PIN and biometrics;
- encrypted local database;
- encrypted local backup and restore;
- offline operation;
- accessibility support;
- Slovenian, Croatian and English.

## Excluded

- user accounts;
- cloud storage;
- cross-device synchronization;
- multiple remote caregivers;
- subscriptions;
- advertisements;
- AI medical advice;
- OCR;
- barcode scanning;
- medicine interaction checking;
- diagnosis;
- prescriptions;
- doctors and pharmacies;
- Apple Health;
- Health Connect;
- smart watches;
- SMS and email reminders.

## MVP discipline

MVP means fewer features, not weaker architecture.

New ideas are recorded as future candidates and do not interrupt the approved implementation sequence.

See [[Care Circle Work Package Register]] for the controlled sequence in which this scope is being implemented.
